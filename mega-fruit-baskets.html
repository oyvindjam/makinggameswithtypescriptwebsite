Mega fruit baskets

Our game is called Mega fruit baskets and is heavily inspired by the excellent game Super Crate Box made by Vlambeer.

Mega fruit baskets is an action game where you pick up fruit baskets to earn points while avoiding the fruit monsters. Each time you pick up a fruit basket, you equip a new weapon.
